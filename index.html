1. Visibility of system status

Critical
Observation

Enrollment is paused, but buttons still suggest immediate registration, creating a broken expectation loop.

UX Impact

High bounce rate and erosion of brand trust for first-time visitors seeking help.

Recommendation

"Switch to Maintenance/Info mode: explain the pause and capture intent via notification signup."

Trust Infrastructure

The psychological gap between "data monitoring" and "health support" must be bridged. We recommend a permanent trust banner in the footer or near account actions.

Proposed Messaging:

"Your V-safe responses are confidential and used strictly for vaccine-safety monitoring. Security protocols are in place to protect the platform, ensuring your personal health data remains private."
