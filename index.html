Detailed Heuristic Findings

High Impact Medium Impact

1. Visibility of system status

Critical
Observation

Enrollment is paused, but buttons still suggest immediate registration, creating a broken expectation loop.

UX Impact

High bounce rate and erosion of brand trust for first-time visitors seeking help.

Recommendation

"Switch to Maintenance/Info mode: explain the pause and capture intent via notification signup."

2. Match between system and real world

Critical
Observation

Privacy language is heavy on technical/security jargon rather than healthcare reassurance.

UX Impact

Users may feel 'monitored' rather than 'cared for,' leading to defensive data entry.

Recommendation

"Introduce plain-language microcopy: 'Your data helps keep everyone safe—here is how we protect it.'"

3. Error prevention & recovery

Serious
Observation

Verification codes and vaccine entry involve multi-step precision without enough 'safety nets'.

UX Impact

Higher support ticket volume and abandonment due to technical setup friction.

Recommendation

"Implement inline validation, 'Save for later' options, and clearer 'Resend Code' prompts."

Trust Infrastructure

The psychological gap between "data monitoring" and "health support" must be bridged. We recommend a permanent trust banner in the footer or near account actions.

Proposed Messaging:

"Your V-safe responses are confidential and used strictly for vaccine-safety monitoring. Security protocols are in place to protect the platform, ensuring your personal health data remains private."
